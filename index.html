J&D Sheet Metal Solution is a trusted name in precision sheet metal manufacturing, delivering high-quality Laser Cutting, CNC Bending, Sheet Metal Fabrication, and Job Work services. We combine advanced technology with skilled craftsmanship to produce accurate, durable, and cost-effective solutions for industries of all sizes.

Our state-of-the-art laser cutting machines ensure clean and precise cutting for Mild Steel, Stainless Steel, and Aluminium. Our CNC bending facility provides highly accurate bends that meet exact customer specifications.

From custom fabrication projects to large-scale production, we are committed to quality, timely delivery, and complete customer satisfaction. Every project is handled with professionalism, precision, and attention to detail.
